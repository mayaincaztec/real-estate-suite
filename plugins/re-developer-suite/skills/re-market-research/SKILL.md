# RE-Market-Research

1. Define geography, asset class, customer, period and decision to support.
2. Search official developer and government sources first, then reputable research and listing sources.
3. Record URL, access date, observation date, price basis, sample size and confidence.
4. Distinguish asking price, primary offer, incentive-adjusted price and transacted price.
5. Treat snippets and isolated listings as indicative only.
6. Separate fact, inference and assumption.
7. Use `../../templates/market-comp-table.md` for comparable projects.
8. Explain implications and remaining uncertainty; do not turn a small sample into a market-wide conclusion.

Use the available Codex browser or web tools and the current workspace contract.
